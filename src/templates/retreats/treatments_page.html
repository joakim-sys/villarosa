Deep Tissue Massage - 55 mins

A therapeutic technique that targets deeper layers of muscles and connective tissue to alleviate chronic tension, pain, and stiffness. Through slow, deliberate strokes and firm pressure, we can reach underlying muscle fibers, tendons, and fascia, promoting relaxation and releasing stubborn knots and adhesions. This intensive form of massage therapy can provide relief from conditions such as chronic back pain, sports injuries, and repetitive strain injuries. It’s often sought after by individuals seeking relief from persistent muscle tightness or those undergoing rehabilitation from injuries. Deep tissue massage not only eases physical discomfort but also promotes improved circulation and mobility, leaving clients feeling rejuvenated and restored.
