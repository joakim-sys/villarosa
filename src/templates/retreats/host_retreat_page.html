Host a Retreat

Peace and quiet, healthy, whole foods, time to focus on feeling your best.
Our retreat offers the refreshing getaway we all need. Our mission is to provide a supportive
space for individuals to nurture their sustainable wellbeing.

Imagine spending time with a group of like-minded people in a beautiful setting, where you can work on developing your business or brand, participate in motivational workshops, find inspiration, share meals and most importantly, have fun.

This experience can be enjoyed as a retreat attendee, but why not take it a step further and host your own event? There is a growing trend, particularly among wellness bloggers, to host gatherings that foster connections.

Many connections are made through social media, but opportunities for in-person connections are becoming fewer. A retreat offers a way to break away from this trend by providing an escape that not only nurtures a sense of community but also facilitates personal and professional growth.
Group retreats unite inspiring educators, passionate participants, and dedicated professionals for an enriching journey of learning, discovery, and personal growth.

If you’re looking to increase engagement with your wellness community and expand your network, we’d love to help you get started.

Accommodation & Facilities

Featuring 11 bedrooms, 10 bathrooms, and numerous inviting lounge areas, our venue presents an ideal setting for hosting your retreat with confidence. The retreat exudes a warm and positive vibe, boasting a variety of inviting spaces for relaxation and meaningful interactions.

  • 10 Superking or 20 twin beds
  • 3 Sets of bunk beds
  • Hairdryers in rooms
  • Beach, bath and hand towels
  • Bed linen provided
  • Optional maid service
  • Private chefs and catering
  • Treatments and therapies
  • Excursions/Activities
  • 16 x 8 mtr Swimming Pool
  • Tennis Court
  • French Boules
  • Yoga room, with props
  • Outdoor Yoga Platform
  • Gymnassium
  • Table tennis & pool table
  • Outdoor kitchen and bar
  • Sunbeds and cabana beds
  • Central heating throughout
  • Air conditioning in all bedrooms
  • Log burner and pellet heater

To make your experience even more seamless, we offer a complimentary planning service, supporting and assisting you throughout your retreat planning process. Your vision is our priority, and we are committed to creating an unforgettable and successful retreat for you and your guests.

  • Up to 12 passengers €173 Each Way
  • Up to 16 passengers €187 Each Way
  • Up to 20 passengers €205 Each Way
  • Up to 24 passengers €222 Each Way

Prices are based on a private minibus, not shared with other passengers.
